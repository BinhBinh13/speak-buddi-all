## Quyet dinh thiet ke

1. **question_type dung VARCHAR(30) CHECK** (khong dung PostgreSQL ENUM): nhat quan voi pattern cua `users.role`, `users.status`, `topic.source` trong schema_core.sql va schema_learning.sql. De ALTER sau khi them loai moi.

2. **Seed quiz_answer dung subquery + NOT EXISTS toan-table**: thay vi WHERE NOT EXISTS(SELECT 1 FROM quiz_answer WHERE answer_text = v.answer_text) — vi answer_text co the trung nhau giua cac cau hoi khac nhau, nen guard bang `NOT EXISTS (SELECT 1 FROM quiz_answer qa WHERE qa.quiz_question_id = q.id)` de chong duplicate cho toan bo cau hoi.

3. **get_questions_by_test dung 2 query + in-memory join**: tranh N+1 bang cach lay tat ca answers trong 1 query voi `ANY(CAST(:qids AS UUID[]))`, sau do ghep vao dict Python. Pattern nhat quan voi cach xu ly trong `repositories/user_repo.py` (dung raw SQL thay ORM).

4. **upsert_attempt_answer dung ON CONFLICT DO UPDATE**: PostgreSQL UPSERT an toan, khong can check truoc. Snapshot `is_correct` duoc truyen tu tang service — service goi `check_fill_blank_answer()` truoc khi goi repo.

5. **Seed grammar_mapping: is_correct=TRUE cho tat ca 4 dong**: vi grammar_mapping la dang noi tu-nghia, tat ca 4 cap deu la dap an dung (moi cau hoi co the co nhieu dap an dung).
